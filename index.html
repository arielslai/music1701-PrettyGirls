Welcome!

 

This project explores Reneé Rapp’s “Pretty Girls” as a nuanced portrayal of queer identity and the emotional complexities of bicuriosity. Through analysis of the song’s lyrics, live performance, and visual storytelling, it examines how Rapp captures both the thrill and vulnerability of sapphic experiences. Supporting materials—a live performance review, the music video analysis, an essay on LGBTQ+ advocacy, and curated playlist—place the song within broader conversations on queer representation in pop music and pop culture. Ultimately, the project highlights “Pretty Girls” as a powerful contribution to emotionally resonant queer storytelling in mainstream media.
